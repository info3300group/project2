2010~2017 NYC Rat Sightings

By Adeniyi Fagbewesa, Elise Song, Ka-Hyun Nam, and Morgan Suh

This interactive visualization plots a cloropleth map of Average Property Sales per Year, as well as the number of Rat Sightings per Year for each NYC borough.

Directions: Interact with the slider to adjust the year. Click to zoom into a borough and double-click to zoom back out to the city.
